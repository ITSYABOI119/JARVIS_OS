=== ISSUES FLAGGED BY ALL 7 JUDGES ===

1. tinyllama-1.1b: Non-functional. Echo + [end of text] on most prompts. Infinite repetition on P5.
2. Qwen3-4B: Generates 8-10 follow-up questions instead of answering. Pathological instruct tuning.
3. Qwen3.5-4B: <think> blocks leak into output. Fatal for embedded use.
4. Llama-3.1-8B: "PowerPoint PPT Presentation" training data contamination in P3, P6 responses.
5. gemma-4-E4B: Internal "Self-Correction/Refinement" notes leak into output (P6, P7).
6. Phi-3-mini: <|assistant|> token leaks. P2 and P9 generated zero content.

=== KEY INSIGHT ===

Gemma 4 E2B (4.65B params, 2.88 GiB Q4_K_M) delivers:
- 56% higher quality than the best Llama model (8.40 vs 5.37)
- 18% faster generation than Llama 3.2 3B (19.7 vs 16.7 tok/s)
- In a smaller file (2.88 GiB vs 4.58 GiB for Llama 3.1 8B)

The ~1000 LOC engine work (PLE + shared KV + dual RoPE + sliding window)
is the highest-ROI investment remaining in Phase 3c. It unlocks:
- The #1 ranked model for IDLE and ACTIVE tiers
- The entire Gemma 4 family (E2B + E4B)
- Architectural features (sliding window, shared KV) used by future models
